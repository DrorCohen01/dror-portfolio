How I Work

6-Step Design Process

A structured process that ensures consistent results and satisfaction every step of the way

01

Strategy

Understanding the business, target audience, competitors, and success metrics. We define together what the product needs to achieve and who it's for — before drawing a single line.

02

Scope & Requirements

Defining required features and prioritizing by business value and resources. What's in the MVP, what's in v2, and what can we cut altogether.

03

Information Architecture

Mapping navigation, page structure, and user flows. Sitemaps and user flows that ensure the user always knows where they are and where they can go.

04

Wireframes

The skeleton of the product — layout, hierarchy, and content without visual distractions. A phase that lets us test assumptions quickly and save on change costs later.

05

Visual Design

Turning wireframes into polished screens with colors, typography, animations, and micro-interactions. Figma prototypes that feel like the real product.

06

Iteration & Learning

Continuous improvement based on feedback, A/B tests, and analytics. Good design doesn't end at launch — it evolves with the product.
